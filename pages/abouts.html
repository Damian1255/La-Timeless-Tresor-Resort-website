Our History

We were founded in 2001 by Damian Lau. He envisioned a resort that fulfilled all senses. Le timeless Tressor is a leading family, leisure resort in Le Classique Baie. It is an integrated resort more then 5000 5-star hotel rooms, suites, villas, and mansions across multiple locations on our island. World-class celebrity chef restaurants, largest casino, state of the art museum, complex golf course. It has an intimate setting designed, to relax and rejuvenate your senses, so that every moment at Le Timeless Tresor is a unique memory.

Getting to our island

Explore La Baie Classique by coming by on our complementary ferry service. Take our ferry from Harbourfront port to our island. You will just need to show your hotel confirmation to our staff to board the ferry or you could pay a $10 admission fee per private guest.
