Le Timeless Trésor

Rising Comfort to the Highest level

Enjoy your stay in paradise

A world away from the everyday, Le Timeless Trésor creates the time and space for you to rediscover the meaning of relaxation, well taken care of by our attentive yet discreet hospitality. Luxuriate in the privacy of the resort’s beachfront and over-water villas that offer an oasis just steps from the sand, or the pleasure of slipping straight from your deck into the cool, clear waters to reach your own private lagoon. We offer 7 different kinds of lux room. All the rooms are specially designed by Muji's original Art Director, Ikko Tanaka is a Japanese graphic design legend.

Our History

We were founded in 2001 by Damain Lau. He envisioned a resort that fulfill all senses.We hold the highest quality of services for you. Le timeless Tressor is a leading family, leisure resort in Le Classique Baie.It is a integrated resort more then 5000 5-star hotel rooms,suites, villas and mansions across multiple locations on our island. World class celebrity chef restraurants, largest casino, state of the art museum ,complex golf course. It has intimate setting designed, to relax and rejuvenate your senses, so that every moment at Le Timeless Tresor is a unique memory.
